Agora Hackathon Philippines 2026:
Build Next Gen AI Sales Agent
​✨ About the Event

​🎙️ Agora Hackathon Philippines 2026: Build Next Gen AI Sales Agent is a builder-focused hackathon in Manila where innovators, developers, and founders come together to create the next generation of AI-powered sales solutions. Participants will design and prototype intelligent sales agents that can automate workflows, personalize customer interactions, and transform how businesses engage with customers. With the help of cutting-edge AI tools, real-time communication technologies, and collaborative mentorship, teams can move rapidly from idea to working prototype—turning bold ideas into impactful solutions.

​👥 Who should join
Developers, startup builders, professionals, students, and tech enthusiasts with basic programming knowledge who want to explore real-time Voice AI and sales-focused AI applications.

​🛠️ What you will do
📘 Learn the fundamentals of building voice-enabled AI sales agents, including the core components and a simple starter flow
🧑‍🏫 Build and improve your project during the hackathon with mentor support and guided checkpoints
🤝 Collaborate with your team, test ideas quickly, and deliver a working prototype demo by the end of the event

​🌟 Why it matters
You will build practical, real-time AI solutions using Agora, while connecting with builders in Manila 🇵🇭. You will also get to see how other teams approach sales agent use cases, exchange ideas, and grow your network in the local developer and startup community 🔗.
