# Gaps

1. No Native CRM Integration
Agora's agent can qualify leads, but there's no built-in bridge to push data into Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive in real time. Your agent gathers gold (intent signals, prospect responses) but drops it on the floor. Build: a live CRM sync layer that writes lead scores, call summaries, and next steps directly into a CRM record after each call.

2. No Lead Scoring During the Call
The agent qualifies leads, but doesn't produce a BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) score in real time that a human sales rep can see on a dashboard. Build: a live scoring panel that updates as the conversation progresses, so reps can decide when to intervene.

3. No Post-Call Intelligence Layer
After a call ends, nothing happens automatically. There's no auto-generated call summary, no sentiment report, no follow-up email drafted, no action items logged. Build: a post-call pipeline that uses the transcript to generate a CRM note, a follow-up email, and a lead score — automatically.

4. No Calendar / Booking Integration
The agent can "schedule meetings through natural conversations," but there's no native integration with Google Calendar, Calendly, or Outlook to actually book the slot in real time and send confirmations. Build: a live booking flow inside the voice conversation.
